About Us

Who We Are

E-Commerce is guided by four principles: customer obsession rather than competitor focus, passion for invention, commitment to operational excellence, and long-term thinking. E-Commerce strives to be Earth’s most customer-centric company, Earth’s best employer, and Earth’s safest place to work. Customer reviews, 1-Click shopping, personalized recommendations, Prime, Fulfillment by E-Commerce, AWS, Kindle Direct Publishing, Kindle, Career Choice, Fire tablets, Fire TV, E-Commerce Echo, Alexa, Just Walk Out technology, E-Commerce Studios, and The Climate Pledge are some of the things pioneered by E-Commerce.

What We Do

Although our business has evolved over the years, one constant is customers’ desire for lower prices, better selection, and convenient services. Today, E-Commerce shoppers can find what they are looking for online and in person. From delivering fresh produce to their doorstep to creating and distributing movies, music, and more, we are always finding new ways to delight our customers.

Our Workplace

The customer is at the heart of everything we do. E-Commerce is a place where smart, passionate people obsess over customers and innovate on their behalf. We also invest in employees’ success. E-Commerce will spend over $700 million to provide free skills training to employees—helping them further their careers in tech and in-demand roles such as cloud computing.

Our Impact

In January 2020, E-Ommerce announced an investment of $1 billion in India and committed to digitally enable 10 million micro, small, and medium enterprises, drive ecommerce exports worth $10 billion, and create 1 million additional jobs in India between 2020 and 2025. Since then, E-Commerce has added 300,000 direct and indirect jobs in India and added 250,000 new sellers, putting it well on track to fulfill these commitments. We also actively work to help communities by responding to urgent needs like disaster relief, and we invest in education for children and young adults.

Entrepreneur and e-commerce pioneer Manan Patel is the founder and CEO of the company E-Commerce, owner of The Washington Post and founder of the space exploration company Blue Origin. His successful business ventures have made him one of the richest people in the world.
